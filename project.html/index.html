About

He is the 1st indian to become the CEO of Alphabet.Sundar Pichai, whose full name is Pichai Sundararajan, is an Indian-American business executive who has made significant contributions to the technology industry. He was born on June 10, 1972, in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India. Sundar Pichai is best known for his association with Google, where he has held various key positions, including his role as CEO.
